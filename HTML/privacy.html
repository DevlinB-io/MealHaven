Data Protection and Privacy Overview

MealHaven values your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) and international data standards such as GDPR.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal details such as your name, email address, and account preferences during registration. Usage data like recipe selections and pantry items are also recorded to improve recommendations.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data enables MealHaven to deliver personalized features, maintain security, and enhance performance. We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored on secure servers with encryption and restricted access. Passwords are always encrypted before storage.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies to improve functionality and collect analytics on feature usage. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by contacting us.

Contact Us:
Email: support@mealhaven.com
Response Time: Within 48 hours

6. Data Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of MealHaven after changes means you accept the updated terms.
